About Spring Stainless Steel

A Legacy of Metallurgical Precision

For three decades, Spring Stainless Steel has set the standard for high-tensile wire forming and flat metal spring manufacturing. We operate out of a 50,000 sq. ft. purpose-built facility equipped with the latest CNC multi-axis forming machines.

Our expertise spans from Grade 304 austenitic steel to specialized 17-7 PH precipitation-hardened alloys, ensuring your components withstand extreme temperature fluctuations and corrosive oxidative environments.
