Who We Are

Founded in 1995, Spring Stainless Steel has evolved from a specialized metallurgical workshop into a global powerhouse for high-precision wire forming and metal stamping. We serve as the primary supplier for mission-critical components in the aerospace, medical, and renewable energy sectors.

Our facility houses 40+ multi-axis CNC machines and a dedicated heat-treatment laboratory, allowing us to maintain a vertical production chain from raw material analysis to final load certification.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in stainless steel innovation, providing sustainable and durable solutions for a modern world.

Our Mission

To deliver unmatched quality and reliability through precision engineering and customer-centric service.

Our Journey

Three Decades of Engineering Evolution

1995

The Spark

Spring Stainless Steel is founded as a boutique wire-forming workshop in a 2,000 sq. ft. garage.

2004

CNC Revolution

Acquisition of the first multi-axis CNC forming centers, expanding into precision medical components.

2012

Global Expansion

Opening of our dedicated 50,000 sq. ft. manufacturing hub and entry into the North American market.

2020

Aerospace Shift

Achieved AS9100D certification, becoming a tier-1 supplier for global aerospace and defense agencies.

2026

The Future

Pioneering AI-driven predictive maintenance and additive manufacturing for custom spring solutions.
